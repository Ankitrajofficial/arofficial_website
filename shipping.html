Shipping Policy

Thank you for visiting and shopping at WAKE UP WITH YOUR PURPOSE. The following terms and conditions constitute our Shipping Policy.

Digital Product Delivery

All of our digital products (such as e-books, online courses, or downloadable content) are delivered electronically.

After your purchase has been successfully processed, you will typically receive an email with a download link or access instructions within a few minutes. If you do not receive this email, please check your spam folder before contacting us.

There are no shipping fees for digital products.

Processing Time: All orders are processed within 8 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in the shipment of your order, we will contact you via email.
